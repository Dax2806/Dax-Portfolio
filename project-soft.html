Mobile UI / UX Design

SOFT: Cleaning Services

Completed: June 13, 2026

Project Overview

SOFT is a mobile platform that connects users with trusted service providers, starting with home cleaning services. The primary goal is to make booking services fast, simple, and reliable — essentially allowing users to access services “at their fingertips.”

Over time, the platform is designed to scale and expand beyond cleaning into a broader range of home and potentially commercial services.

Target Audience

The primary users are busy homeowners and working professionals who value convenience, reliability, and trust. They are looking for quick, seamless booking without complexity.

Interactive Screen Gallery

Below is a slider featuring the high-fidelity mockups of the core application flow, including the Splash Screen, Service Categories, and User Profile & Bookings.

Designed Screens

  • Splash & Login Screen: Trust-evoking entry point with a clean blue-and-white aesthetic.
  • Home Screen: Immediate access to quick bookings and featured services.
  • Services (4 Screens): Detailed categories for Deep Clean, Standard Clean, Move In/Out, and custom requests.
  • Bookings Screen: Seamlessly track upcoming and past service appointments.
  • Profile Screen: Manage payment methods, addresses, and user preferences.
